Motor Scooter Restoration

How a kid like me makes $400 a week in his spare time

How is it Done?

  1. Scour KSL Classifieds, Facebook Marketplace, Ebay, etc...
    • Must be in good shape cosmetically
    • Below $200
    • The seller must have a title for it
  2. Buy the Scooter
    • Settle on a price and offer to pick it up that same day
    • Bring a truck, a ramp, and ratchet straps
    • Bargain to get a good deal
  3. Begin Restoration
    • Check if electrics work
    • See if it has spark
    • Clean the carburetor
    • Put new gas in it
    • Check the oil
    • Check the hoses/lines
    • Consult YouTube
  4. Take it for a Ride
    • Drive it for at least 20 miles
    • Fine tune it
  5. Clean it
    • Wash it all down with water and soap
    • Remove all rust
    • Clean the gauges
    • Dry with a towel
  6. Take Photos & Post it
    • Get some pretty looking photos
    • Post it online
    • List all the benefits and amenities
    • List the problems
  7. Sell it!
    • Strike a deal with a buyer
    • Pay your tithing
